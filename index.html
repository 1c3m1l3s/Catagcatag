My Story

I am a second-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Cebu Technological University – Main Campus. Ever since my first day at CTU, I’ve been driven by curiosity—how systems work, how programs come to life, and how technology shapes the world around us. What started as simple interest slowly grew into passion as I explored programming, design, and problem-solving in deeper ways.
